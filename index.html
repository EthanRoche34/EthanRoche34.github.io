The Accessibility Of Cybersecurity

Commercial Title

Ethan Roche Woodward

Student Number: 20094256

Project Description: Cybersecurity is an ever-growing field in the world, especially with the rapid adoption of Artificial Intelligence and the subsequent challenges it poses. This project explores the history of cybersecurity, its tools, and its current state from the perspective of someone with limited computer knowledge. Understanding cybersecurity is crucial in maintaining the security of personal devices, which raises the question: How easy is it for the average user to grasp the concepts of cybersecurity?
