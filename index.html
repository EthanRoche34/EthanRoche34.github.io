CyberClarity: Navigating Digital Safety for Everyone

Ethan Roche Woodward

Student Number: 20094256

Project Description:

Cybersecurity is a critical and expanding field in today's digital world, especially with the rapid integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the new challenges it introduces. This project delves into the evolution of cybersecurity, exploring its key tools and its current landscape, all from a perspective accessible to those with minimal computer experience. Given the increasing reliance on internet-connected devices, it's essential for everyone to have a basic understanding of cybersecurity principles.

Understanding cybersecurity isn't just about protecting data; it's about safeguarding our digital lifestyle from potential threats. This raises an important question: How can the average user, who might not be tech-savvy, easily understand and apply cybersecurity concepts to protect their personal devices?
